How we work with you

We learn the business first.

NamEngine starts with what you are naming, who it is for, and the market signals the name needs to carry.

Then you set the taste.

Tell NamEngine what kind of names you naturally respond to: trusted, modern, premium, bold, familiar, or distinctive.

We invent around your taste.

Each name is created by NamEngine and tuned to the style you described, instead of pulled from a standard business-name list.

You can keep nudging.

If the first set is close but not quite right, tune it softer, more classic, more modern, or more distinctive.

We screen for rarity.

Names are checked against known name lists so the results feel original, not accidentally common. Absolute global uniqueness cannot be guaranteed.

Your taste stays private.

We use your naming notes to shape your list. You decide if and when to share it.
